Tartu: The city of diversity

My Experience in Tartu

Aura Waterpark

The Aura Waterpark in Tartu is a popular indoor water park that offers a fun and exciting experience for visitors of all ages. The water park features several pools, slides, and water attractions, including a wave pool, a lazy river, and a variety of water slides. There are also several saunas and steam rooms available for those who want to relax and unwind. The park is a great place to spend a day with family and friends, and there are plenty of amenities available, including restaurants, cafes, and locker rooms. Overall, the Aura Waterpark in Tartu is a must-visit attraction for anyone looking for a fun and memorable aquatic experience.

Estonian National Museum

The Estonian National Museum in Tartu is a fascinating cultural institution that showcases the history and heritage of Estonia. The museum features a wide range of exhibits and displays, including artifacts, documents, photographs, and multimedia presentations. Visitors can learn about the country's history, culture, and traditions, as well as its people and their way of life. The museum also offers educational programs and events, such as workshops, lectures, and guided tours, which provide a deeper understanding of Estonian culture and history. Overall, the Estonian National Museum in Tartu is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in learning more about this beautiful and fascinating country.

AHHAA Science Centre

The AHHAA Science Centre in Tartu is a fun and interactive museum that offers visitors a hands-on experience with science and technology. The museum features a variety of exhibits and displays that explore topics such as physics, biology, chemistry, and astronomy. Visitors can participate in a range of activities, from building robots and launching rockets to exploring the mysteries of the human brain. The museum is especially popular with children, who can learn about science and technology in a fun and engaging way. Additionally, the AHHAA Science Centre offers educational programs and workshops, as well as special events and exhibitions throughout the year. Overall, the AHHAA Science Centre is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in science and technology, and a fun and educational experience for visitors of all ages.
